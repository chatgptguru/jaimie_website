About Us

At Jaimie Coe - HR & Safety, we design, implement, and oversee compliance at work.

My company is an Australian owned and managed providing integrated compliance solutions to businesses throughout Australia, New Zealand and beyond. My team of highly qualified and experienced risk management consultants provide our clients with simple yet comprehensive integrated risk management systems that foster higher stakeholder engagement, and improve legal compliance. We specialise in customised paperless Integrated Risk Management Systems that comply with Federal and State legislation and are robust, reliable, and relevant to each client’s unique requirements. We do more than develop and implement risk management systems. We believe in long-term relationships, providing ongoing support, advice and incident response manage for our clients.
